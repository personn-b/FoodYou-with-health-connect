- 🚀 **Health Connect Sync (Fork Exclusive)** – Fully integrated via **AI implementation** to seamlessly sync your nutrition and data with **Google Fit**, **Open Vitals**, and other Health Connect compatible platforms.
- 🔒 **Privacy First** – No account required, all data stored locally on your device (Health Connect data stays securely on-device too).
- 🧩 **Modular Home Screen** – Customize your home view with functional cards that suit your habits.
- 📚 **Comprehensive Food Databases** – Seamlessly integrates Open Food Facts, USDA FoodData Central, and Swiss Food Composition Database.
- 🧪 **Full Nutrition Tracking** – Set and track personalized nutrition targets, monitoring not only calories and macros but also vitamins, minerals, and other essential nutrients.
- 🍲 **Recipe Creation** – Create custom recipes by combining foods, with nutrition calculated instantly.
- 🎨 **Material You Design** – Adaptive theming and modern UI.
